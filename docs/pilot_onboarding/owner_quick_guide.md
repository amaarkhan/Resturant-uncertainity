# Restaurant Uncertainty MVP: Owner Quick Guide

Welcome to the Kitchen Portal! This smart AI-driven prep tool analyzes external factors (like weather and local events) to tell you exactly how much of your menu to prepare every single morning, minimizing your food waste and preventing stockouts.

## 🌅 The Morning Routine

1. **Sign In**: Navigate to your provided Kitchen Portal URL (`http://...:5173`) and log in using your Kitchen Manager credentials.
2. **Review Context**: On the *Daily Plan* tab, check the "Auto-Detect Weather & Events" toggle. Ensure it is checked so the system can grab real-time forecasts for your area automatically.
3. **Generate Suggestion**: Click the strong primary button labeled **"Generate Suggestion"**.
4. **Follow the Prep**: The AI will output an exact *Recommended Qty* for each of your items.
   * If a `Reason` says "Adjusted by Heavy Rain," understand that foot traffic is predicted to drop, saving you money on wasted food!
   * You can accept this recommendation blindly or manually deviate. 

## 🌇 The Evening Routine

At the end of your shift, you must log what *actually* happened. This feedback trains the AI!

1. **Submit Daily Outcomes**: Scroll down to the *End of Day Reality Check* table.
2. **Enter the Math**: For every item, verify how much you *actually* Prepared, how many were Sold, and if you experienced a Stockout (meaning you could have sold more but ran out).
3. **Submit**: Click "Submit End of Day Metrics."
4. **Quick Feedback Box**: (Optional) Provide a quick 1-5 confidence rating on how the application performed today and leave a brief context note if something weird happened (e.g., "Road was blocked due to construction").

## 📈 Tracking Your Success
Want to see your waste drop over time? Click the **14-Day History** tab at the top of your dashboard to view intuitive graphs showing exactly how many items you threw away vs how many you sold successfully over the last two weeks.

> [!TIP]
> **Trust the AI:** Early pilots prove that owners who adhere closely to the generated recommendations see an average 15% reduction in weekly waste!
